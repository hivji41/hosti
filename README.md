## What Is Hosti, Exactly?

Hosti (hosti.us) is a web hosting company founded in October 2018 — originally under the name Quality Hosting, which later rebranded to Hosti. They run their own infrastructure out of INAP data centers in Dallas, Texas, with a 1Gbps network, 20Gbps DDoS protection, and panels powered by Plesk (web hosting) and Pterodactyl (game hosting).

The team is small and fully remote, which honestly is pretty standard for this tier of hosting. They're not trying to be AWS. They're trying to give you a solid VPS or shared host that doesn't go down every Tuesday afternoon.

One customer summed it up well: *"Quality Hosting [Now Hosti] is by far the best hosting company when it comes to VPS's. The service comes at a reasonable price for great specs."*

## Who Should Use the VPS?

Honestly? The **Large plan** is the sweet spot for most people who know what they're doing with a server. Two cores and 4GB of RAM is plenty for:

- A self-hosted WordPress or Ghost blog getting decent traffic
- A Node.js or Python app backend
- A Discord bot server
- A small game server (though for games, their dedicated Game Hosting is worth a look)
- A VPN or reverse proxy setup

The **Small plan** at $2.99/month is a legitimate steal for low-traffic projects, dev environments, or just playing around with a Linux server. At that price, it's basically the cost of a coffee.

The **Extra Large** makes sense when you're running something genuinely demanding — a busy community server, a heavier web application, or multiple services on a single machine.
